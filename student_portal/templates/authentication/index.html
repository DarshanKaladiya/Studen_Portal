About Friendmates

Friendmates is an innovative online platform designed for students, teachers, and mentors to connect, share resources, and enhance their academic journey. Our goal is to create a collaborative learning environment where students can find study partners, access educational materials, and seek guidance from experienced mentors.

Whether you're preparing for exams, looking for study groups, or need expert advice, Friendmates makes learning interactive and enjoyable.

Key Features

Study Groups

Find and join study groups based on subjects, interests, and schedules.

Resource Sharing

Upload and access verified notes, guides, and materials curated by mentors.
